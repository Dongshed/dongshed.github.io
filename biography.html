Biography

Dongsheng Ding is a PhD candidate in the Ming Hsieh Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Southern California. He received the BE in Automation and ME in Control Theory and Engineering from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2011 and 2015, and the MS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, in 2017. His research interests broadly lie in optimization and control. His primary focus is the analysis and design of control or decision-making algorithms for dealing with real-world environments, e.g., safety or risk constraints, multi-agent interactions, and unknown or time-varying dynamics.
