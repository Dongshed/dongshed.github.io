About me

I am a fifth-year PhD student in the Ming Hsieh Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Southern California. I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Mihailo R. Jovanovic. I received my BE in Automation and ME in Control Theory and Engineering form Zhejiang University, my MS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. My research interests lie in Optimization, Control Theory, Reinforcement Learning, and their interfaces.
