About me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Electrical and Systems Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania under the supervision of Professor Alejandro Ribeiro, and an incoming Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Tennessee, beginning in Fall 2025. I received my PhD in Electrical Engineering from the University of Southern California under the supervision of Professor Mihailo R. Jovanovic. I received my BE in Automation and ME in Control Theory and Engineering from Zhejiang University, and my MS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. My research focuses on application of principles and tools in optimization and control to the study of requirement-driven machine decision-making.
