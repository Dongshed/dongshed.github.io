About me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Electrical and Systems Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania under the supervision of Professor Alejandro Ribeiro. I received my BE in Automation and ME in Control Theory and Engineering from Zhejiang University, my MS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, and my PhD in Electrical Engineering from the University of Southern California under the supervision of Professor Mihailo R. Jovanovic. My research interests lie in Optimization, Control Theory, Reinforcement Learning, and their interfaces.
